Hisor

Situated 25km from Dushanbe, the Hisor Fortress is one of the best preserved historical sites in Tajikistan. Nearby, there are some ancient schools and a mosque.

Karakul

Karakul Lake is surrounded by snow mountains. Its clear water reflects the reflection of the mountains forming a beautiful vista. Try to stay in one of the Yurts (traditional tents) to experience the nomadic life of the Tajik people who herd their livestock here every summer.
